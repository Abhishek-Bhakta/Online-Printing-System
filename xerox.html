Blog: Printouts delivered in minutes

Ever needed to print out an assignment or boarding pass or even visa application forms at the last minute only to realise that the ink in the printer has turned into a piece of rock. Yes, we relate to the pain as well.

In August 2022, Blinkit launched printing services in select areas of Delhi NCR. The need was very selfish - we all felt that sporadic printing should not be a burden that occupies permanent space in our lives only to fail us when we really need it. It also helped that our existing distribution network enabled us to deliver high quality printouts to our customers at their doorsteps in minutes

Why we built this service I have bought three printers...
It was a constant struggle to keep them functional. I spent more time troubleshooting and fixing issues than actually using them. That’s when we realized that providing a reliable and quick printing solution was not just about convenience but also about reducing stress and saving time for our users.

Frequently Asked Questions
What is the return policy?
Our return policy allows you to return items within 30 days of receipt. Please ensure that the item is in its original condition and packaging.
How can I track my order?
You can track your order using the tracking number provided in your confirmation email. Visit our tracking page and enter the tracking number to get real-time updates.
Do you offer international shipping?
Yes, we offer international shipping. Please check our shipping policy for more details on delivery times and rates to specific countries.
